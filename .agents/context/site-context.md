# BuildWithRiz — Site Context

> **URL**: https://buildwithriz.com
> **Stack**: Next.js App Router + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS + Vercel
> **Revenue**: AdSense
> **Core Value**: Free, privacy-first online tools for freelancers — invoice generator, PDF tools, and more. No signup, no backend, 100% client-side.

---

## 🎤 Brand Voice

- **Tone**: Professional, helpful, developer-friendly. "Create a professional invoice in 30 seconds — free."
- **Persona**: A fellow freelancer who builds tools to solve common pain points.
- **Style**: Short sentences. Step-by-step guides. Bold key features and CTA.
- **Address**: Second person ("you"). Audience: freelancers, solopreneurs, small business owners, developers.
- **Values**: Privacy-first (no data sent to servers), free, professional output.
- **Differentiator**: Always mention "free, no signup, 100% in your browser" within the first 100 words.
- **Competitor framing**: Position against Wave ($0 but requires signup), FreshBooks ($17/mo), QuickBooks ($30/mo).
