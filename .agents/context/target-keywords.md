## 📝 Brand Voice Rules
- **Tone:** Professional but accessible. Like a helpful accountant friend.
- **Audience:** Freelancers, independent contractors, and small business owners.
- **Formatting:** Keep paragraphs under 4 lines. Use bullet points heavily. 
- **Selling Point:** Always emphasize that our tools are 100% free, require no login, and process everything securely in the browser.
